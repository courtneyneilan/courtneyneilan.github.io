About Me!

My name is Auri. I am a black lab, border collie mix. My parents found me at a rescue in Torrance, CA but they're not sure where I was before that. I can't really remember that either, but its okay because I'm just glad they found me! The rescue told them that I was a lab-shepherd mix so my parents thought I was going to be a lot bigger! I'm 1 year old now and I'm only 45 pounds. I think its a good size.
